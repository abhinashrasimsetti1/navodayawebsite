Process

How to Apply

Admission to Class VI is through the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test (JNVST). Follow these steps:

01

Online Registration

Visit the official NVS website and register during the application window (November-December). Provide basic details and select your district.

02

JNVST Examination

Appear for the two-hour test consisting of Mental Ability Test (MAT), Arithmetic Test (AT), and Language Test (LT). Held annually in February.

03

Result & Selection

Results are published on nvs.edu.in. Selection is based on merit with reservations for SC/ST/OBC and rural candidates as per government norms.

04

Document Verification

Visit the allotted JNV with original documents: birth certificate, domicile, caste certificate, passport photos, and previous school records.

05

Join the Vidyalaya

Report to the school in April. All expenses including tuition, boarding, lodging, and textbooks are completely free under the NVS scheme.
