About JNV

Shaping Bright Minds Since 1986

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas are a system of residential schools in India providing free education to gifted children from all backgrounds. Established under the National Policy on Education, JNVs represent one of the largest and most successful educational initiatives in the world.

With over 650 schools across the country, we identify talent through a fair entrance examination — giving every child, regardless of economic background, access to world-class education.

Admissions

Your Journey Begins Here

Admission through JNVST — a fair, transparent entrance examination conducted annually for Class VI entry.

01

Online Registration

Register on the official NVS portal during November-December. The process is free and open to all eligible candidates.

02

JNVST Examination

A two-hour test covering Mental Ability, Arithmetic, and Language. Conducted in February across thousands of exam centers.

03

Result & Counselling

Merit-based selection with government reservation norms. Results published on nvs.edu.in with school allotment.

04

Document Verification

Submit original documents at the allotted JNV for final confirmation of admission.

05

Welcome to JNV

Join the residential program in April. All costs — tuition, boarding, lodging, books — are completely free.
